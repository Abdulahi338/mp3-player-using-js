A restaurant business is a type of business that prepares and serves food and drinks to customers. Meals are typically eaten
on the premises, but many restaurants also offer takeout and delivery services. Restaurants can vary greatly in appearance and offerings,
including a wide variety of cuisines and service models.

Fine dining

Fine dining: These restaurants typically offer upscale cuisine and a formal dining experience.
They may have a dress code, and the service is usually very attentive

Fast food:

These restaurants offer quick and affordable meals. They typically have a limited menu of pre-made items, and the service is very fast.

Family-style

These restaurants offer a family-friendly atmosphere and often have large portions. They may also have a buffet or all-you-can-eat options.

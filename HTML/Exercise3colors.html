SOM Sports

Somalia is a nation where sports play a vital role in everyday life. Football (soccer) reigns supreme, with both men and women enjoying the beautiful game. However, Somali sports extend far beyond the pitch. Traditional activities like camel racing and archery hold cultural significance, while modern sports like basketball and track and field are gaining traction. Somali athletes like Abdi Bile, a decorated runner, have brought international recognition to the country. Despite facing challenges, Somalia's sporting scene thrives on passion and a commitment to athletic excellence.
